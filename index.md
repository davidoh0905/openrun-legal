# OpenRun Privacy Policy

**Effective July 19, 2026**

OpenRun helps people coordinate temporary, in-person runs.

## Information OpenRun collects

OpenRun may process:

- Your Google or Apple account identifier
- Your name and optional provider profile image
- Hosted-run details, including time, pace, distance, group size, notes, and precise meeting location
- Join requests and coordination messages
- Device push-notification tokens
- Safety reports and blocked-runner records

## How information is used

OpenRun uses this information only to:

- Display temporary run invitations
- Verify run and request ownership
- Coordinate in-person runs
- Deliver relevant notifications
- Prevent abuse and respond to safety reports
- Provide user support

OpenRun does not sell personal information and does not use advertising trackers.

## Information visible to others

While a run invitation is open, people browsing OpenRun may see its title, host’s first name, schedule, pace, distance, group count, notes, and meeting location.

Join requests and private messages are limited to the requester and host. Run Room messages are limited to the host and accepted runners.

## Service providers

OpenRun uses:

- Supabase for authentication, database, realtime updates, and server functions
- Apple and Google for sign-in
- Expo for application builds and push-notification delivery

These providers process information under their respective privacy terms.

## Retention and deletion

Public invitations disappear after their scheduling window. Coordination rooms close after a six-hour grace period.

You may permanently delete your OpenRun account from Settings. Account deletion removes your profile, hosted runs, join requests, coordination messages, and registered push devices. Safety records may be retained where reasonably necessary to investigate abuse or comply with legal obligations.

## Your choices

You may:

- Decline notification permission
- Sign out
- Block another runner
- Report a run or message
- Delete your account

You may also manage Google or Apple account access through those providers.
